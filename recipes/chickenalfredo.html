Chicken Alfredo

Wanting an easy Italian dish on a weekday? This is the dish for you! This recipe will guide you through making a creamy and delicious meal, perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of chicken
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 1 box of penne rigate
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • 1 cup of italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Place the chicken in the bottom of the crock pot.
  2. Place the heavy cream, chicken broth, and butter in a small bowl and mix them together.
  3. Pour the mixture into your crock pot.
  4. Add the seasonings to the crock pot.
  5. Cover and set your crock pot to high.
  6. Cook for four hours or until chicken is done.
  7. Remove the chicken from the crock pot.
  8. Pour the box of pasta into the crock pot and cook for 30 minutes.
  9. Shred the chicken and place to the side while the pasta cooks.
  10. Once the pasta is done, mix the chicken back in.
  11. Once mixed, the chicken alfredo is ready to enjoy!
