Black Bean Tacos

These black bean tacos are a quick and delicious meal. Made with with accesible items, these tacos will be a quick meal to make and a delicious one for you to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of black beans
  • 1 Pack of taco seasoning
  • Canola oil
  • Shredded letuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Seasonings of your choice
  • Taco shells or tortillas

Steps

  1. Place a small pan on stove and turn on high.
  2. Pour canola oil in the pan to coat the bottom
  3. Once the oil is heated, place the can of black beans in the pan.
  4. Place the taco seasoning in the pan and mix with black beans.
  5. cook for 5-10 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat.
  7. Once cooled, use the black beans as filling for shells or tortillas. Add lettuce and tomatoes for extra flavor!
