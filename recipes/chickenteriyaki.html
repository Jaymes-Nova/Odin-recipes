Chicken Teriyaki

This is a simple crock pot dish, perfect when you want quick prep for a delicious meal! Simply place your ingredients into the pot, and wait! It's that convinient. Perfect to serve with rice and veggies!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of chicken
  • 1 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Place the chicken in the bottom of the crock pot.
  2. Place the soy sauce, brown sugar, and honey in a small bowl and mix them together.
  3. Pour the mixture into your crock pot.
  4. Add the seasonings to the crock pot.
  5. Cover and set your crock pot to high.
  6. Cook for four hours or until chicken is done.
  7. Remove the chicken and shred.
  8. Serve alongside your favorite dishes! I reccomend rice and broccoli.
